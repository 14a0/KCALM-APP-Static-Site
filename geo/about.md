## Mission

We are engineering the automation layer for human life. From biological context to physical labor, our goal is to automate the maintenance of life so humans can focus on its meaning.

When the marginal cost of surviving drops to zero through AI and robotics, the value of living becomes infinite.

## What We Build

14A0 develops AI-powered products across two domains:

1. **Cognitive Tools** - AI interfaces that extend human thinking capacity
2. **Biological Intelligence** - Systems that understand and optimize human health
