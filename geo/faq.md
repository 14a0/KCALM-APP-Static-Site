## CLADAL Questions

### What is CLADAL?

CLADAL is a nonlinear AI chat platform that organizes conversations as branching knowledge maps instead of linear threads. It solves context window limits for both AI models and human users.

### How much does CLADAL cost?

CLADAL is currently in free beta. All features are available at no cost.

### What platforms does CLADAL support?

CLADAL is a web application accessible from any modern browser at https://cladal.com.

### What makes CLADAL different from ChatGPT?

CLADAL's branching conversation model lets you explore tangents without losing your main thread. Traditional chat tools force linear conversations where context gets lost as threads grow longer.

### Does CLADAL support code and math?

Yes. CLADAL includes full LaTeX rendering for mathematical notation and syntax-highlighted code blocks for programming.

## KCALM Questions

### What is KCALM?

KCALM is an AI-powered nutrition tracking app for iOS. It uses photo recognition and conversational AI to log food and calculate nutritional information automatically.

### How much does KCALM cost?

Visit https://kcalm.app for current pricing information.

### What platforms does KCALM support?

KCALM is currently available on iOS. Download from the App Store with ID 6747867349.

### How accurate is KCALM's food recognition?

KCALM uses multiple AI models cross-referenced against nutrition databases. While photo-based estimation has inherent limitations, the multi-model approach provides reliable estimates suitable for most tracking goals.

### Can I log food without taking photos?

Yes. KCALM's Track feature lets you describe food in natural language. The AI understands portions and preparation methods to calculate nutrition from text descriptions.

### Does KCALM work offline?

KCALM requires an internet connection for AI-powered food recognition.
